# Design Partner Outreach — v1.1

**Status**: Template  
**Goal**: Recruit 5 design partners for v1.1 roadmap shaping  
**Channel**: Email / Twitter DM / LinkedIn DM

---

## Who we're looking for

- You run AI agents in production (OpenCode, Codex, Claude Code, or custom)
- Your team has 2–50 engineers
- You're currently solving orchestration, observability, or governance yourself with custom tooling
- You're willing to do 2–3 one-hour calls over 6 weeks to shape v1.1

---

## What you get

- **Direct influence** on v1.1 features: skill bundles, approval gates, multi-region fallback
- **Free access** to the control surface during the design partner period (价值 $500/month)
- **Dedicated support** from our team during your onboarding
- **Co-author credit** in the v1.1 release notes if your feedback materially shapes a feature

---

## What we ask

1. **Intro call (30 min)** — Walk us through your current AI infrastructure setup
2. **Design review (60 min)** — Review v1.1 proposals, give us candid feedback
3. **Usability test (60 min)** — Try the new features as we build them, think aloud
4. **Summary call (30 min)** — Overall impressions, roadmap priorities

All calls are async-friendly where possible. We work across timezones.

## Qualifying Questions

Use these to qualify a lead before sending the full pitch:

1. "How many AI agents are you running in production today?"
2. "What's your current approach to orchestration — custom scripts, a framework, or something else?"
3. "When something breaks in your AI pipeline, how do you find out about it?"
4. "What's your biggest operational pain point right now — observability, reliability, cost, or something else?"

**Pass criteria**: Running 3+ agents OR significant custom orchestration overhead.
